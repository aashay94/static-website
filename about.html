TRAVEL INDIA

Nurtured from the seed of a single great idea - to empower the traveller - Travel India went on to pioneer the entire online travel industry in India. Travel India has revolutionised the travel industry over the years. This is the story of Travel India, India’s Online Travel Leader. Travel India, India’s leading online travel company was founded in the year 2000 by Deep Kalra. Created to empower the Indian traveller with instant booking and comprehensive choices, the company began its journey in the US-India travel market. It aimed to offer a range of best-value products and services along with cutting-edge technology and dedicated round-the-clock customer support.
